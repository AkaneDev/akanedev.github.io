How Likely?

Find out how secure your system is with this app.

This tool performs 15 basic security checks to help you identify potential vulnerabilities. It’s simple, straightforward, and avoids unnecessary complexity.

However, it’s important to note that this app does not guarantee complete safety. It’s not a virus scanner or a comprehensive security solution. Instead, it focuses on basic checks to ensure your system is configured securely and doesn’t leave you with a false sense of security.

The goal is to pass all 15 checks and achieve a perfect score of 15/15. Here’s an example of the output:
